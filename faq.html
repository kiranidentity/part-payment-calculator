Common Questions

Clarifying your doubts about loan prepayments.

1. Do banks charge a penalty for part payments?

For floating rate individual home loans, RBI has mandated zero prepayment penalties. However, for fixed-rate loans or business loans, banks may charge a small fee (usually 0.5% - 2%). Always check your loan agreement.

2. Is it better to reduce EMI or Tenure?

Without a doubt, reducing Tenure is financially superior. Reducing tenure saves you lakhs in compound interest. Reducing EMI primarily helps with monthly cash flow but doesn't offer significant long-term savings.

3. How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator uses standard amortization logic used by most Indian banks. However, exact figures may vary slightly due to difference in days-count conventions (360 vs 365 days) used by different lenders.

4. When is the best time to make a part payment?

The best time is as early as possible. Interest is highest in the initial years of the loan. A pre-payment made in the 1st year saves 5x more money than the same amount paid in the 15th year.

5. Is my data safe?

Yes. This calculator runs entirely on your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your financial details remain 100% private and on your device.

6. Can I use this for Car Loans or Personal Loans?

Absolutely. While the example uses a Home Loan, the math works the same for Personal and Car loans. Just enter your specific Interest Rate (e.g., 10-14% for Car, 11-20% for Personal).
